## Description

> Three intelligence services. Three artefacts. One week in March 1989.
>
> MI6, CIA, and KGB each produced one artefact during the same period. These materials
> have been collected for a joint exercise codenamed **CONCORDAT**.
>
> Your starting point is `https://welbournesecurity.com/`.
>
> The intercepts are hidden within the site. Find them, recover each fragment, and
> assemble the final flag at the operator workstation. Each leg yields one fragment.

## Objective

Find and decode the three transmissions, one British (WAVERLY), one American (SOLO),
one Soviet (KURYAKIN), recover a word fragment from each, and assemble the combined
flag at the operator workstation.

## Provided files

None directly. Every artefact is hosted on the site and reached through a hidden trail
that begins on the Concordat page.
